Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna is an italian pasta dish. It consists of layers of pasta sheets, tomato sauce, meat sauce, and cheese. It is then baked and served hot.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Meat Sauce

Steps

  1. Cook Pasta
  2. Cook Meat Sauce
  3. Assemble Lasagna Layers
  4. Cook
  5. Serve
